- Yozaru voicebanks 🎤 -

Presentation:

Yozaru (from ヨザル meaning Night monkey) is a masculine Utau voicebank created by Yoazur avaliable in multiple languages.
It has 3 voice colors : Natural, Soft and Solid for all voicebanks. It is highly recommended to use these voicebanks using OpenUtau for ease of use. Sounds best between C3-A4, but is versatile with high pitched songs.

Character description:

Yozaru is a teenager who loves music and singing. He can be shy but he becomes more confident when he sings. He is however quite lazy and is often sleepy, almost nocturnal (explaining the meaning of his name). That said, he is smart and he has experience in computer science. Fun fact, he uses a walkman to listen to music, but he doesn't really want people to know that. Can you tell i'm running out of things to say about him?
